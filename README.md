## ðŸ“Š Dashboard Insights

### 1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The current year data shows a general decline in accidents and casualties compared to the previous year:
* **Total Casualties:** 195.7K (decreased by **11.9%**).
* **Total Accidents:** 144.4K (decreased by **11.7%**).
* **Fatal Casualties:** 2.9K, showing a significant drop of **33.3%**.
* **Serious Casualties:** 27.0K (down 16.2%).
* **Slight Casualties:** 165.8K (down 10.6%).

### 2. Vehicle & Road Analysis
* **Vehicle Involvement:**
    * **Cars** are involved in the vast majority of casualties (**155,804**), followed by **Vans** (15,905) and **Bikes** (15,610).
    * Agricultural vehicles account for a minor portion (399).
* **Road Type:**
    * **Single Carriageways** have the highest number of casualties, significantly higher than Dual Carriageways or Roundabouts.

### 3. Environmental & Geographic Factors
* **Urban vs. Rural:**
    * The majority of casualties occur in **Urban areas (61.95%)** compared to Rural areas (38.05%).
* **Light Conditions:**
    * **Daytime** accidents account for **73.84%** of casualties, while 26.16% occur in the Dark.
* **Location:** The analysis covers the United Kingdom, highlighting major hubs like **Glasgow, Dublin, Manchester, and Birmingham**.

Based on the data, the following safety measures are recommended:
